Why This Exists

The Problem

Every time you want a quick Tic Tac Toe game, you face the same wall: mandatory account creation, intrusive ads, slow-loading apps, and UI buried under feature bloat.

  • ✕ Sign up just to play a 2-minute game
  • ✕ Ads interrupting every round
  • ✕ Cluttered UI hiding the actual game
  • ✕ No score memory across rounds

The Solution

Tic Tac Toe — a pure, browser-based game that loads instantly, tracks your score, and gets out of your way. Open the tab. Play. That's the entire flow.

  • ✓ Zero signup — open and play instantly
  • ✓ No ads, no popups, no distractions
  • ✓ Clear board, highlighted winning line
  • ✓ Running score across multiple rounds
